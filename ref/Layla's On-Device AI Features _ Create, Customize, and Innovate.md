## Explore Layla's On-Device AI Features

![onboarding-ezgif.com-optimize (1).gif](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/5f294b_b86dd48f697448d9b978ddc4669a8506~mv2.gif)

Choose from tailored use cases for your on-device AI.

You can use Layla as your personal assistant, friend, roleplay partner, and more!

All information you share with Layla stays on your device.

![IMG_6047.PNG](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/5f294b_4b2193ce86a24f0d8d39441d950540c0~mv2.png/v1/fill/w_310,h_618,al_c,q_85,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/IMG_6047_PNG.png)

Add different features to Layla by adding "mini-apps".

Each app has its own self-contained features you can use to enhance your experience with Layla.

We are adding more "apps" every week, from frivolous ones such as checking your horoscope, to more serious technological advances such as local text-to-speech with over 100+ voices!

![IMG_6050.PNG](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/5f294b_2d0a3f12cbc443eda0d0409fc19f9ac2~mv2.png/v1/crop/x_0,y_103,w_1179,h_2350/fill/w_310,h_618,al_c,q_85,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/IMG_6050_PNG.png)

Engage in multi-character roleplay!

Create your own scenarios and characters for Layla to act out. You can also participate in the story as one of the characters, choosing to move the story forward in any direction you wish!

All information stays on your phone so your imagination is your limit!

![presetchars-ezgif.com-optimize (1).gif](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/5f294b_0653f57e1faf45c8951aa2f7613cffd4~mv2.gif)

Discover and download new characters from personality hub!

These characters are created by our users!

Layla can take the form of any character you wish.

Once you download, all information becomes local to your device.

You can also choose to create your own characters and share them with the world anonymously!

![imggeneration-ezgif.com-optimize.gif](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/5f294b_ba468d5537fb4c23ac032071786c3b53~mv2.gif)

Image generation with mobile-optimised Stable Diffusion models.

You can run stable diffusion models on your phone, generating images during chatting for a more immersive experience!

![IMG_6049.PNG](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/5f294b_218666353cca42e79330d68d90cb2ce3~mv2.png/v1/fill/w_310,h_618,al_c,q_85,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/IMG_6049_PNG.png)

Customise advanced settings for Layla if you are technical.

If you are a local LLM enthusiast, we offer a wide range of customisable settings to tailor your experience.

From setting your own temperature that controls how creative Layla is, to loading your own LLM models from your own sources, we've got you covered.

![A women scientist working on a male android](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/68cffa_a2686c376aa34e9783b72bfe415f43f6~mv2.png/v1/crop/x_44,y_0,w_935,h_1024/fill/w_295,h_323,al_c,q_85,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/12.png)

On-device Processing

The neural network runs completely on your phone. It uses the latest consumer CPUs and GPUs to process your requests.

![interior of a sci-fi sleep area](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/68cffa_ab2bde2d44964b1ba1d4aac60acefca8~mv2.png/v1/crop/x_44,y_0,w_935,h_1024/fill/w_295,h_323,al_c,q_85,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/priavcy.png)

Enhanced Privacy

All your data remains on your device. Your conversation histories, preferences, and data is not sent to anyone unless with your express permission.

We make it very clear what data is sent and what remains on your phone.

![satellite](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/68cffa_3ee5520c64ca4b7ebebde5bba53119fa~mv2.png/v1/crop/x_44,y_0,w_935,h_1024/fill/w_295,h_323,al_c,q_85,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,enc_avif,quality_auto/satellite.png)

Constant Innovation

We provide updates to base models and personalities regularly. The updates are independent of your data; you can keep using Layla offline with peace of mind.
